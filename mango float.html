Mango Float

Ingredients Procedure
  • 3–4 ripe mangoes (sliced thinly)
  • 1 pack graham crackers
  • 2 cups all-purpose cream (chilled overnight)
  • 1 can (300 ml) condensed milk
  1. In a bowl, combine the chilled all-purpose cream and condensed milk. Mix well until smooth and slightly thick.
  2. Peel the mangoes and slice them thinly. Set aside.
  3. Arrange a layer of graham crackers in a rectangular container.
  4. Spread a layer of the cream mixture over the crackers.
  5. Add a layer of sliced mangoes.
  6. Repeat the layers (graham crackers, cream, mangoes) until the container is full.
  7. Finish with cream and mangoes on top.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for 4–6 hours or overnight until firm.
  9. Slice and serve chilled.

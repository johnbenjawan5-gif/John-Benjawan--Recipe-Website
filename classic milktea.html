Classic Milktea

Ingredients Procedure
  • Tea: 2 bags of Black Tea (Earl Grey or Ceylon work best).
  • Liquid: 1 cup Water and ½ cup Whole Milk (or half-and-half for extra creaminess).
  • Sweetener: 1–2 tbsp Brown Sugar or Honey (adjust to taste).
  • Ice: 1 cup.
  • Optional: Cooked Tapioca Pearls (Boba).
  1. Brew: Steep tea bags in 1 cup of boiling water for 5 minutes. You want it extra strong since the milk and ice will dilute it.
  2. Sweeten: Remove tea bags and stir in your sugar while the tea is still hot so it dissolves completely.
  3. Cool: Let the tea sit for a few minutes, or pop it in the fridge to reach room temperature.
  4. Combine: In a shaker (or a jar with a tight lid), add the tea, milk, and ice.
  5. Shake: Shake vigorously for 30 seconds. This aerates the milk and creates a frothy, smooth mouthfeel.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass (over boba pearls if using) and enjoy.

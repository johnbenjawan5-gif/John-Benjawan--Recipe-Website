Garlic Bread

Ingredients Procedure
  • Bread: 1 loaf of French bread or a Baguette, halved lengthwise.
  • Butter: ½ cup Unsalted butter (softened to room temperature).
  • Garlic: 3–4 cloves Fresh garlic, minced (or 1 tsp garlic powder).
  • Herbs: 2 tbsp Fresh parsley, finely chopped.
  • Seasoning: ½ tsp Salt (adjust if using salted butter).
  • Optional: ¼ cup Grated Parmesan or shredded Mozzarella.
  1. Preheat: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Make Garlic Butter: In a small bowl, mash the softened butter, minced garlic, parsley, and salt together with a fork until smooth.
  3. Spread: Generously slather the butter mixture onto the cut sides of the bread, ensuring it reaches the very edges.
  4. Bake: Place bread (buttered side up) on a baking sheet. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the edges are golden and crisp.
  5. Broil (Optional): For an extra-crunchy top or to melt cheese, turn the broiler on for the last 1–2 minutes. Watch closely—it burns fast!.
  6. Slice: Let it cool for a minute, then slice into 2-inch thick pieces.

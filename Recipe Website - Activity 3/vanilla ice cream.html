Ingredients Procedure
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  1. In a bowl, whisk together the sugar, milk, and salt until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions (usually 20–25 minutes).
  4. Transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 2 hours to firm up.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

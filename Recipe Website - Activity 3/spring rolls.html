Ingredients Procedure
  • ½ kilo ground pork
  • 1 small carrot (finely chopped)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 20–25 spring roll wrappers
  • Cooking oil (for frying)
  1. In a bowl, combine ground pork, carrot, onion, and garlic.
  2. Add egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Separate the spring roll wrappers.
  4. Place 1–2 tablespoons of meat mixture on one edge of the wrapper.
  5. Roll tightly and seal the edge with water.
  6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  7. Fry the spring rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove from oil and drain excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with sweet chili sauce.

Lemonade

Ingredients Procedure
  • Lemons: 4–6 large Fresh Lemons (about 1 cup of juice).
  • Sweetener: 1 cup Granulated Sugar.
  • Water: 6 cups Cold Water (divided).
  • Ice: 2–3 cups.
  • Optional: Fresh Mint leaves or Lemon slices for garnish.
  1. Make Simple Syrup: In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water. Heat over medium until the sugar is completely dissolved. Let it cool slightly.
  2. Juice Lemons: Roll the lemons on the counter to loosen the juices, then squeeze until you have 1 cup of fresh lemon juice. Strain out any seeds.
  3. Combine: In a large pitcher, stir together the lemon juice, the simple syrup, and the remaining 5 cups of cold water.
  4. Chill: Stir well and taste. If it's too tart, add a splash more water; if it’s too sweet, add more lemon.
  5. Serve: Pour into glasses filled with ice. Add mint or lemon slices for a fancy finish.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients Procedure
  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • 200g bacon or pancetta (sliced)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove of garlic (minced)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  1. Cook the Spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instruction.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add bacon and cook until crispy.
  4. Whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with bacon.
  6. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper
  8. Serve immediately with extra cheese on top.

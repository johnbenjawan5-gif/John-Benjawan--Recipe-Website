Beef Steak

Ingredients Procedure
  • 500 g beef sirloin or tenderloin (sliced thinly)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 medium onion (sliced into rings)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup water (for simmering)
  1. In a bowl, combine beef slices, soy sauce, calamansi/lemon juice, black pepper, and sugar. Let it marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a pan. Sauté garlic until fragrant. Add onion rings and cook until soft. Remove onions and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the marinated beef. Cook for 3–5 minutes until browne.
  4. Add 1/2 cup water to the beef and simmer for 10–15 minutes until tender.
  5. Add the cooked onions on top of the beef. Stir gently.
  6. Add sugar (optional) if you want slightly sweet. Let the sauce thicken.
  7. Transfer to a plate and serve with steamed rice.

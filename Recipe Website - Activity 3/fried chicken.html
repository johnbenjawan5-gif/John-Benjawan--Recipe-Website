Crispy Fried Chicken

Ingredients Procedure
  • Chicken: 2 lbs (thighs or drumsticks work best for juiciness).
  • The Soak: 2 cups Buttermilk + 1 tsp Salt + 1 tsp Hot sauce.
  • The Coating: 2 cups All-purpose flour + ½ cup Cornstarch (the secret to extra crunch).
  • The Seasoning: 1 tbsp each of Garlic powder, Onion powder, Paprika, and Black pepper.
  • Oil: Vegetable, Canola, or Peanut oil (for frying).
  1. Marinate: Soak chicken in the buttermilk mixture for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours in the fridge). This tenderizes the meat.
  2. Prep Flour: In a shallow bowl, whisk together the flour, cornstarch, and all seasoning.
  3. The Double Dredge: * Take a piece of chicken from the buttermilk, let the excess drip off.
  4. Heat Oil: Fill a heavy pot with 2 inches of oil. Heat to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry: Carefully lower chicken into the oil. Fry for 12–15 minutes, turning halfway, until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the skin is golden brown.
  6. Rest: Let the chicken rest on a wire rack (not a paper towel) for 5 minutes so it stays crunchy.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients Procedure
  • 2 cups Sugar.
  • 2 large Eggs.
  • 1 cup Whole milk.
  • ½ cup Vegetable oil.
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract.
  • 1 cup Boiling water.
  • 1 ¾ cups All-purpose flour.
  • ¾ cup Unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking powder.
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking soda.
  • 1 tsp Salt.
  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry: In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add Wet: Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes.
  4. Bake: Pour evenly into pans. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool: Let cool in pans for 10 minutes, then move to wire racks to cool completely before frosting.

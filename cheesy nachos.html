Cheesy Nachos

Ingredients Procedure
  • 1 large pack of tortilla chips (around 200–250 g).
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a mix).
  • ½ cup jalapeño slices (optional, for spice).
  • ½ cup black olives (sliced).
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped.
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped (seeds removed).
  • ¼ cup corn kernels (optional).
  • Salt and black pepper to taste.
  1. Preheat: Set oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Layer: On a sheet pan, do half the chips, half the cheese, and half the protein. Repeat for a second layer.
  3. Bake: Heat for 5–8 minutes until the cheese is bubbly.
  4. Top: Add fresh veggies (tomatoes, onions) and cold dollops (sour cream, guac) just before serving.

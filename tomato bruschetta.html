Ingredients Procedure
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 baguette or Italian bread, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Place the bread slices on a baking sheet and brush lightly with olive oil.
  3. Toast the bread in the oven for 5–7 minutes until golden and crisp.
  4. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes to blend the flavors.
  6. Top each toasted bread slice with the tomato mixture.
  7. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Serve immediately as an appetizer.

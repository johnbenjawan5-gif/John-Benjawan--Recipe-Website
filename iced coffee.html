Iced Coffee

Ingredients Procedure
  • Coffee: 1 cup Strong Brewed Coffee (use double the grounds if brewing hot) or Cold Brew.
  • Sweetener: 1–2 tbsp Simple Syrup or Sugar (syrup dissolves better in cold drinks).
  • Creamer: ¼ cup Milk, Half-and-half, or Oat milk.
  • Ice: 1–2 cups Fresh Ice Cubes.
  • Optional: A drop of Vanilla Extract or a pinch of Cinnamon.
  1. Brew Strong: Brew your coffee at double strength (use 4 tablespoons of grounds for 1 cup of water). If using hot coffee, let it sit for 5 minutes to cool slightly.
  2. Sweeten First: Stir your sugar or syrup into the hot coffee before adding ice. This ensures it dissolves completely.
  3. The "Chill" Step: Fill a tall glass to the brim with ice. Pour the warm, sweetened coffee over the ice.
  4. Add Dairy: Pour in your milk or creamer. Watch the "marble" effect as it swirls through the dark coffee.
  5. Final Stir: Give it a quick stir to even out the temperature and flavor.

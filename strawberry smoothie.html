Ingredients Procedure
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • ½ cup yogurt (optional for creaminess)
  • 1–2 tablespoons honey or sugar (to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a chilled smoothie)
  1. Wash the strawberries and remove the stems.
  2. Place strawberries, banana, milk, yogurt, and honey into a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add ice cubes if desired and blend again until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve

Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients Procedure
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs.
  • ½ cup melted butter.
  • 2 tbsp sugar.
  • 2 tbsp sugar.
  • .4 (8 oz) cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar.
  • 1 cup sour cream.
  • .1 tsp vanilla.
  • 3 eggs.
  • 2 cups blueberries.
  • ¼ cup sugar.
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice.
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water.
  1. Crust: Mix crust ingredients, press into 9-inch pan, bake at 325°F (160°C) for 10 minutes. Cool..
  2. Filling: Beat cream cheese and sugar. Add sour cream, vanilla, then eggs (one at a time). Pour over crust.
  3. Bake: 55–70 minutes until center slightly jiggly. Cool, then chill 4 hours.
  4. Topping: Cook all topping ingredients until thick. Cool and spread over cheesecake.
  5. Chill and Serve.
